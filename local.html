FAQ

Common questions.

How long does it take to build my website?

Most sites launch in 2 weeks from the day we receive your content. Rush delivery (1 week) available for +$400.

Do I have to pay a monthly fee?

No — the monthly plans are optional. You can take our managed hosting (from $25/month, with SSL, backups, and support included) or host the site anywhere you like. You always own the code and the domain.

What's the difference between hosting with you vs. hosting it myself?

Hosting with us means we handle SSL, daily backups, security patches, uptime monitoring, and you have someone to call when something breaks. Hosting it yourself costs ~$15–25/month through providers like Netlify, Vercel, or SiteGround — cheaper, but you're on your own for issues.

Can I update the website myself later?

Yes. Growth and Premium tiers include a simple CMS so you can change text, images, hours, and services without touching code.

What if I don't have logos or photos?

We'll work with what you have and fill gaps with professional stock imagery. Logo design available as an add-on for $250.

What if I already have a domain or old website?

We'll use your existing domain. If you have an old site, we can migrate your content — just share the login or URL.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes — see our Hosting & Care plans. Managed Care ($75/mo) includes updates, backups, security patches, and an hour of content changes every month.
